UCVTS.org


Front page

Overview

UCVTS.org is the official site of the UCVTS school district. The site contains useful information about the district including a link to PowerSchool, a link to the school webmail, a way to apply to the school, a lunch schedule, a schedule for school events, and a staff directory. The main page also contains links to the official pages of the different programs the district offers. All in all, the site is put together in a pretty easy to navigate, intuitive way drastically improving upon the old site in these regards.

Visually, the main issue with the site is the front page. As soon as the viewer opens the page, they see a huge slideshow that takes up the entire screen. Upon scrolling down, you will see that the images and links are crowded together. If these were spaced out a little more, the site would be significantly easier to look at.
